How to Contact Register2Park Customer Service

If you experience any problems with your parking registration, permit, or enforcement, Register2Park offers dedicated customer support to assist you.

Register2Park Customer Service Phone Number

  • Availability: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (local time)

Calling this number connects you to the Register2Park support team who can help with:

  • Vehicle registration assistance

  • Clarification of parking rules and policies

  • Troubleshooting account or technical issues

  • General parking inquiries

Email Support

This option is ideal for non-urgent questions or when you need to send documents or screenshots.

What to Have Ready When You Call

Providing clear and complete information helps the support team address your concern efficiently.

What to Do If Your Vehicle Was Towed

Register2Park itself does not operate towing services but works with property managers and towing companies. If your vehicle was towed, here's what you need to know:

  • Towing Service Phone Number: (905) 873-7100 Ext. 304

  • Contact the towing company directly for towing-related inquiries, fees, and vehicle recovery.

  • Have your registration details and towing notice handy when calling.

If you believe your vehicle was towed unfairly, customer service can guide you on next steps but cannot directly release vehicles.
